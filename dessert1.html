Beaver Tails

Earlier Origin



It is considered a famous dessert in Canada, and Beaver Tails' snack shops
can be seen in many tourist areas. It is a fried pasta created in Ottawa,
made from whole wheat flour. The reason why it is called a beaver's tail is because
it is shaped like the tail of a representative animal in Canada called Beaver.
The beaver tail can be sprinkled with different seasonings, such as cinnamon and sugar,
chocolate hazelnuts, maple butter, or apple cinnamon. It has a variety of
flavors and a crispy bottom, making it popular among Canadians and tourists.
