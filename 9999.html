Gravy and Cheese Fries

Gravy and cheese fries are a heavenly combination of crispy french fries smothered in rich, savory gravy and melted cheese. This indulgent dish is a comfort food favorite that tantalizes the taste buds with its perfect blend of flavors and textures.

How It's Made

The magic begins with golden, crispy fries that are cooked to perfection. These fries serve as the delicious base for the toppings that follow.

Next, a generous ladle of warm, flavorful gravy is poured over the fries. The gravy adds a savory depth that perfectly complements the crispiness of the fries.

Lastly, a generous sprinkle of melted cheese is added, providing a creamy and gooey texture that brings the dish together in the most delightful way.

History of Gravy and Cheese Fries

Poutine is a Quebec food made from fresh-cut French fries and cheese curds and gravy. It first appeared in rural Quebec snack bars in the 1950s. It became widely popular throughout Canada in the 1990s. Poutine can be found everywhere from fine dining menus at fancy restaurants to fast food chains such as McDonald's and Burger King. It has become an iconic symbol of Quebec cuisine and culture.
