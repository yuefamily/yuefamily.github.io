Canada Bacon

Bacon is derived from the English word "bacon", and its original meaning is smoked rib meat (i.e. square meat) or smoked salted back meat. Bacon is one of the three main types of western meat products (ham, sausage), its flavor in addition to salty taste, but also has a strong smoky flavor. Bacon skin oil is golden yellow, leather is hard, with the finger hit a light "porphyria" sound; Lean meat is dark brown, hard and dry, and bright when cut.

How make bacons

Step 1: Put all the seasonings into a dish and mix them slightly

Step 2: After cleaning the surface of the pork belly, dry the water, put it into the seasoning and knead it well with your hands. Constantly massage the meat to make the seasoning evenly wrapped on the surface of the meat

Step 3: Store in a ziplock bag and refrigerate for up to 3 days

Step 4: After 3 days, remove the meat from the ziplock bag and put it directly on the hollowed-out grill into the refrigerator to dry for 3 days. It can be hung outdoors to dry in winter

Step 5: After air drying, remove and wash the spices on the surface and wipe dry

Step 6: It was smoked for about an hour

Step 7: slice

Step 8: If you want to eat, remove without adding oil, directly put in a non-stick pan fry until browned on both sides.
