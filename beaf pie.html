Tourtière - A Canadian Meat Pie

Introduction

Tourtière is a savory meat pie that originates from the French-Canadian culinary tradition. It is a beloved dish often enjoyed during the holiday season and special gatherings.

History

The history of Tourtière can be traced back to the early settlers of Quebec, Canada. It was a hearty and delicious meal that helped sustain the inhabitants during the harsh Canadian winters.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat (usually a combination of pork and beef)
  • Onions
  • Spices (such as cloves, cinnamon, and allspice)
  • Pastry crust (for the pie shell)

Recipe

Here is a basic recipe for making a delicious Tourtière:

  1. Prepare the pastry crust and line a pie dish with it.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground meat and onions until browned.
  3. Add the spices and any additional ingredients according to your family's recipe.
  4. Transfer the meat mixture into the pie shell.
  5. Top with another layer of pastry and seal the edges.
  6. Bake until golden brown and enjoy!

Serving

Tourtière is traditionally served hot and can be accompanied by various condiments like ketchup, chutney, or pickles. It pairs well with mashed potatoes and a simple salad.
